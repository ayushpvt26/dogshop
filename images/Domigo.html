Dog Day Care

Woof - sniff- run- rest - repeat

Dog Day care is like babies play school. A place where you can leave your dog in the morning and pick them up in the evening.

Why to choose a day-care for dogs?

Stay fit & active

Regular activity is good for their physical & mental health, it also helps to drain their excessive energy.

Socializing & friendly

To offer them some social life and a chance to mingle & make friends with other dogs

Build confidence

Exposure to different environmental setting & meeting new dogs and people everyday reduce stress & anxiety & boost confidence.

▼・ᴥ・▼

Playtime at Domigo's day care leads to a happy, healthy, and loving companion.

What a day look like at dog day-care?

Drop them at Domigo

Drop them off in the morning 9-12pm or use our pick n drop service.

Sent for rest and refreshment

After a M&G session they are sent to their respective kennels for some rest & refreshments followed with mid-day meals.

Pick them up

After a good day at day care, pickup your happy & highly satisfied pup in the evening and enjoy relaxing time with them.

Meet to pack members

A brief meet & greet socialization session with their respective pack members in the yard area.

Play with pack members

Evening time again calls for their high energy play session with their respective pack members in the yard area.

How to enroll my dog for a day care?

Pack assessment

Visit with your dog for pack assessment. Pack assessment can be done based on your dog's size & temperament.

Take a free trail

Take a free day-care trail to make your dog comfortable with the place, people and pack.

Select pass

Choose a day-care pass according to your need and see your dog enjoy his days.

Eligibility criteria for a dog day-care

Your pup must be above or 5 months old to avail day-care.
All the vaccination must be up to date including rabies, kennel cough & 9in1.
Deworming must be done in past 3 months.
Must be on any active anti-tick treatment. Preferable treatments should be anti-tick tablet or anti-tick spray.
Avoid female dogs when they are on their periods/heat cycle.
Check-In time in 9am to 12pm.
Check-out time will be 4pm - 7pm, max 8hrs stay.
Charges are calculated according to per day basis.
